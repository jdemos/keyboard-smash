# 🎨 Keyboard Smash!

Press any key, tap, click, or swipe — and watch the magic happen! ✨

Keyboard Smash is a fullscreen game for tiny fingers. Every press, tap, and swipe makes colorful animations explode on the screen. Hours of fun!

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Open the game** → [Play now on GitHub Pages!](https://jdemos.github.io/keyboard-smash/) *(or open `index.html` in your browser)*
2. **Tap or click "Start Playing!"** 🚀
3. **Press any key, tap, click, or swipe** — see what happens! 🌈

That's it! Every key does something different. Try them all!

---

## ⭐ Cool Stuff

- **Fullscreen fun** — the whole screen fills with color and effects!
- **Light & Dark mode** — easy on the eyes, day or night 🌙☀️
- **Star Wars theme** ⚔️ — lightsabers, starfields, and more!
- **Dinosaur theme** 🦕 — roars, volcanoes, and prehistoric creatures!
- **Transportation theme** 🚗 — vehicles, trains, planes, and road signs!

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Grown-ups

- **To exit fullscreen:** Press **Ctrl + Shift + Escape**
- **No installs needed** — it's a simple webpage, just open and play!
- **Sound effects** are on by default — toggle them off with the 🔊 button on the home screen if needed
